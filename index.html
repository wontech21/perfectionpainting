What We Do

Expert Services, Flawless Results

Fully licensed and insured with nearly 20 years of experience. We deliver unprecedented craftsmanship using only the highest quality paints and materials — because your space deserves nothing less.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, and detail work. We protect your home and deliver flawless, even finishes in any color you choose.

Exterior Painting

Comprehensive prep, premium coatings, and weather-resistant finishes that protect and beautify your property for years.

Power Washing

Restore surfaces to like-new condition. Driveways, siding, decks, and walkways — we blast away years of buildup.

Staining & Wood Finishing

Bring out the natural beauty of wood surfaces with expert staining, sealing, and finishing for lasting protection.

Residential & Commercial

From cozy homes to office buildings and retail spaces — we scale our expertise to match your project, large or small.

Decks & Fences

Painting, staining, and refinishing for decks, fences, and outdoor structures. Built to withstand New England weather.

Water Damage Repair

Expert restoration of water-damaged walls, ceilings, and surfaces. We repair, prime, and refinish to eliminate all traces of damage.

Plaster Repair

Seamless plaster patching, skim coating, and restoration. We bring damaged walls and ceilings back to a flawless, smooth finish.

Cabinet Refinishing

Transform your kitchen or bathroom with professionally refinished cabinets. A fraction of the cost of replacement with stunning results.

About Us

Built on Quality.
Driven by Pride.

Perfection Painting was founded by Wendell Nunes with a simple belief: every surface deserves to be treated with care, skill, and respect. With nearly 20 years of hands-on experience, what started as a dedication to flawless finishes has grown into a trusted name across the North Shore and Boston.

We are fully licensed and insured, committed to unprecedented craftsmanship using only the highest quality paints and materials. Whether it's a family home, a commercial storefront, or a weathered deck, we bring the same level of precision and professionalism to every job. Our clients don't just get a paint job — they get the peace of mind that comes with working with someone who treats their property like his own.
